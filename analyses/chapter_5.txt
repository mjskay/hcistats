## Median test for difference in median salary between Team A and B

	Asymptotic Median Test

data:  annualIncome by teamName (Team A, Team B)
Z = -6.5753, p-value = 4.854e-11
alternative hypothesis: true mu is not equal to 0


## Wilcox test for shift in location of salary between Team A and B

	Asymptotic Wilcoxon Mann-Whitney Rank Sum Test

data:  annualIncome by teamName (Team A, Team B)
Z = 7.369, p-value = 1.719e-13
alternative hypothesis: true mu is not equal to 0


## Permutation test for difference in salary between Team A and B

	Asymptotic 2-Sample Permutation Test

data:  annualIncome by teamName (Team A, Team B)
Z = 4.6714, p-value = 2.991e-06
alternative hypothesis: true mu is not equal to 0
